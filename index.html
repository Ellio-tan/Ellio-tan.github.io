About Me

I am a geospatial data analyst, with an academic background in climate change science, public health, and political science. During the course of my professional career, I have worked with various NGOs to help construct an open database made up of reports submitted by oil and gas companies to the Canadian and UK government under the ESTMA act. I am currently based in the San Francisco Bay area working as a GIS contractor on site at a major tech company. My interests primarily lay in data visualization and analysis, focusing on public transit and urban issues, as well as competitive video games.
